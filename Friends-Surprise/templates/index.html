Welcome to "Randomly Surprised" – the ultimate platform for joyous, unexpected moments among friends! Whether you're a fan of spontaneous gifts, heartwarming messages, or delightful experiences, our platform is designed to add a touch of excitement to your relationships.

  • Begin your journey by clicking on the "Sign Up" button prominently displayed on our homepage.
  • Input your essential details – your full name, a valid email address, and a secure password.
  • Hit the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button to submit your registration and officially join the "Randomly Surprised" community.
  • Check your inbox for a verification link. Click on the link to validate your email address and unlock the full potential of surprises.
  • Log in to your newly created account and add the finishing touches to your profile, making your surprises even more personalized.

Building Your Friend Community

Welcome to the heart of "Randomly Surprised" – where friendships flourish, and surprises come to life! Our platform not only connects you with friends but also lets you curate unforgettable moments together.

  • Navigate to the "Find" section on the platform. Use the search feature to look for your friends by their names or email addresses.
  • Once you've found your friends, send them invitations to connect.Click on the "Connect" or "Send Invitation" button next to their profiles.
  • Your friends will receive the invitations and can choose to accept or decline.
  • Once your friends accept the invitations, you are now connected on "Randomly Surprised

Create Event

creating memorable events with your friends is just a few clicks away! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to effortlessly bring your friends together for a surprise-filled occasion

  • Locate and click on the "Create Event" button prominently displayed on the platform.
  • Enter the name of your event in the designated field. Choose the date for the event to occur using the calendar feature.
  • After entering the event details, click on the "Create" button to proceed.
  • You'll be prompted to select friends to include in the event.Browse through your connected friends and choose those you want to invite.
  • After selecting the friends for your event, click on the "Create" button once again to finalize the event details.
  • You'll receive a confirmation message indicating that your event has been successfully created.

  • As the event date approaches, be on the lookout for a notification that will reveal the thrilling information about whom you are set to surprise. It's like unwrapping a gift of anticipation and excitement!
  • Here's the twist – no one, and we mean no one, knows who will be surprising whom until the big reveal. It's the mystery that adds a whole new level of intrigue to your "Randomly Surprised" experience. Get ready to keep everyone guessing!
